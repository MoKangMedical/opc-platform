The OPC Playbook

Distilled wisdom from 18 legends. The framework for building your own one-person company.

Phase 1

Find Your Specific Knowledge

What comes naturally to you but is hard for others? Naval calls it "specific knowledge" — the thing you'd do for free.

"Specific knowledge is found by pursuing your genuine curiosity and passion rather than whatever is hot right now." — Naval Ravikant
Phase 2

Build Leverage

Code, content, or capital. Write code that works while you sleep. Create content that compounds. Invest capital that grows.

"Forget rich vs poor, white collar vs blue collar. It's now leveraged vs unleveraged." — Naval Ravikant
Phase 3

Ship Ugly, Ship Fast

Pieter ships a new product every week. Marc Lou launched 20+ products in 2 years. Perfectionism is the enemy of OPC.

"If you're not embarrassed by the first version of your product, you've launched too late." — Reid Hoffman
Phase 4

Own Your Audience

Build an email list. Grow on Twitter/X. Create a blog. The audience IS the product. Everything else is a monetization layer.

"The best marketing is a great product + honest storytelling." — Jack Butcher
Phase 5

Monetize Without Scaling Headcount

SaaS subscriptions, digital products, courses, books, licensing. Every model that works for OPC has marginal cost approaching zero.

"Charge for the outcome, not your time." — Daniel Vassallo
Phase 6

Stay Small, Stay Sovereign

The goal isn't to build a big company. It's to build a great life. The legends optimize for freedom, not empire.

"Success is when you can stop doing what you dislike." — Derek Sivers
